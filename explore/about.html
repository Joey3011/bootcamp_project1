It All Started in the Village

Joey Coffee House was founded early 2022 by Joeyboy with the simple vision of brewing high quality, unique coffees, and creating a welcoming space for the community.

At Joey Coffee House, we believe the secret to a great coffee experience relies just as much on the quality of coffee as it does the expertise of the barista, which is why we foster and promote the continued education and success of our baristas through industry-leading professional training.

An Unwavering Commitment to Quality

Our sourcing team maintains stringent standards when selecting green coffee for our menu. All beans, from the components of our house selection to our rotating cast of single origin coffees, have met or exceeded our intense quality standards. Joey Coffee House quality protocols require cupping (tasting) hundreds of samples of coffee, maintaining a seasonal rotation of beans, and working with importers and exporters who use the utmost care in transporting and storing the coffee.

Sustainable Relationships

We work to build sustainable and trusting relationships with importers, exports, brokers, and farmers. Working with many of the same individuals each year can guarantee both quality coffee for Joey Coffee House and stability for our wholesale partners. Additionally, relationships with importers and brokers formed on a trusting foundation help us to perennially source only the best coffee from around the world.
