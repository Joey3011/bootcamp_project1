Frequently Asked Questions

ONLINE ORDERING & SHIPPING QUESTION

Where is my order?

We roast orders Monday through Friday and ship the same day. Note that if you order after 7am on any given day, your order will be processed the following roast day. We do this to ensure your coffee arrives to you as fresh as possible!

Where is my order?

As soon as your order ships, we'll email you with a tracking number. If you didn't receive this email, please check your spam folder and email us at shipping@joeycoffeehouse.com if you're unable to locate it.

Once your package leaves our roastery, it's in the hands of the shipping carrier to deliver so we unfortunately do not have any additional information about its whereabouts beyond the tracking number. In rare cases, the carrier may lose your package. If this happens, please get in touch with us so we can file a claim with the carrier and refund/replace your order.

I haven't received my package, but it's been a few days and tracking is pending.

Shipping carriers see a huge amount of shipping volumes throughout the pandemic and during the holiday season. They also have very specific requirements before we can file a claim. Please note that especially during the holidays, there may be delays in shipping that will result in slow tracking updates.

My package says that it was delivered, but I still haven't received it.

If the carrier has marked your package as delivered, but you can't find it, please start by checking with your neighbors, Most of the time, packages turn up this way. Or you can mail us at shipping@joeycoffeehouse.com.

What is your return policy?

Since coffee is a perishable item, and your coffee was roasted to order, we are unable to offer returns on coffee. However, if your order arrived damaged or there was an error with your order, we'd be glad to help! Please contact us for support.

If your package is returned to the roastery, we will contact you, ask questions to resolve the shipping issue (i.e. confirm the address if the return label says “insufficient address”), and offer to reship the same contents of the package.

For all other merchandise, we will happily offer refunds if the item is returned unopened and unused. Contact us to arrange a return.
